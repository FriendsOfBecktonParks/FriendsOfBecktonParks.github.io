Explore Our Parks

Located in one of the most diverse neighbourhoods in the country, Beckton Parks include several football and cricket pitches, a community-led ecology pond amd garden, a wildflower meadow, several community gardens, a woodland area, a farm (currently closed), a lake, and multiple playgrounds, covering approximately 36 hectares of land and two Council wards (Beckton & Custom House).
