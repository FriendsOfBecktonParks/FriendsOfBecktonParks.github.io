Directory

Our aim is to act as an umbrella organisation representing the interests of the wide range of community groups that use Beckton Parks. These include community groups working on conservation and sustainability, children’s education, sport and leisure, health and wellbeing, youth engagement, disability rights and inclusion and religious and cultural services.
